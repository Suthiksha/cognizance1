Pseudocode and flowchart for program that prints Nth fibonacci number:

Declare Integer i,n,a,b,sum
Assign a = 1
Assign b = 1
Assign sum = 0
Output " Enter no. of fibonacci series"
Input n
Assign i = 3
while i <=n
Assign sum = a+b
Assign a = b
Assign b = sum
Assign i = i+1
end
output " sum"
output sum
End
